Another useful pair of elements to separate items are the <div> and </div> tags. These are very commonly used in programming, but they won't be very useful for you until you learn css. Also important are the header tags. These are a set of tags that can be used around some text to make it big and header-like, eg <h1>My Header</h1>. They go all the way from <h1></h1> to <h6></h6>, with h6 being the smallest. Try it out here:
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>

</head>
<body>
  <h1>Big Header or Title</h1>
  <h2>Smaller Header</h2>
  <h6>Smallest Header</h6>
  <div>
    Words bla bla bla
  </div>
  <div>
    More words bla bla bla bla bla
  </div>
</body>
</html>

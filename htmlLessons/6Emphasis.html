Anyone can Code

Some more useful tags to adjust what your words look like are these: <b> and </b>, <i> and </i>, <u> and </u>, and <s> and </s>. Have a look at what each of these tags does below:
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>

</head>
<body>
  <b> Bold </b>
  <i> Italic </i>
  <u> Underlined </u>
  <s> Strikethrough </s>
  <b><u> You can use multiple too! </b></u>
</body>
</html>
